Automatic Shut-Off:

An essential safety feature where after 60 continuous minutes the straightening brush automatically shuts off in case you forget it switched on.

Fast Heat-Up:

It uses 75 watts which allows it to heat up to its maximum temperature of 450℉ (230℃) in just 30 seconds.

Easy-To-Adjust Temperature:

Adjust the temperature according to your hair type with the (+) and (-) buttons. The current temperature will appear on the LCD screen.

LCD Screen:

It displays the temperature as it increases until it reaches the desired heat setting.

Ceramic Bristles:

The ceramic bristles allow you to smoothly straighten your hair faster, more efficiently, and give your hair a shiny finish. They are anti-scald which ensure that no damage is caused to your hair. It is 5-10 times faster than traditional straightening irons.

Silicone Heat-Protection Bubbles:

To ensure maximum safety and to prevent direct contact with the heat, each ceramic bristle ends with a silicon soft bubble. The silicon bubbles facilitate smooth combing, as they also provide a relaxing massage sensation that melts away stress as you stroke your scalp.

Auto-Lock Technology:
